The maths (optional)

Rankings feel decisive because they print a clean order. In real decisions, the scoring process has wobble: raters disagree, criteria get interpreted differently, and the data is incomplete.

RankSmarter turns that into a simple, auditable question: how accurate would your scoring need to be for your cutoff to be guaranteed? If you cannot defend that level of accuracy, the honest move is to treat the boundary as a tie band.
